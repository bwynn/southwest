The Road

Packing over 2000 miles into 4 days means a lot of time spent on the road. It's hard to really grasp how much desert lies between Twentynine Palms and Needles, there were stretches where the road disappeared in the horizon with nothing other than mountains, dirt, sand and shrubs as far as you could see.

Day 1, you're excited. Day 3, you start to feel a little crazy being in the car for such a long time. By day 5, you're numb to the hours getting from one somewhat interesting blip on the map to the next.
